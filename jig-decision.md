# Irish Flute Jig Decisions

| Decision | Selected Path | Why | Open Check |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bore setup | Lathe-supported long drill through square blank | Keeps bore axis as later turning datum | Validate with scrap blank |
| Tone-hole setup | Drill press V-block with foot-end stop | Repeatable hole spacing from foot datum | Build stop after actual body OD known |
| Embouchure workholding | Padded V-block plus hand tools | Allows conservative acoustic refinement | Add drawing after scrap test |
| Tuning workflow | One hole at a time | Prevents irreversible over-opening | Record each step in validation worksheet |
