# Irish Flute Resources And Provenance

The Irish flute is a living traditional instrument associated with Irish dance music and session practice. This packet documents a shop prototype and does not claim to reproduce a specific historic maker's bore, undercutting, or tonal style.

## Model Sources

- instrument-maker-v4.3 `acoustic-models.md`: open-open cylindrical pipe and tone-hole first pass.
- Common D4 flute sanity check: a D4 Irish flute is roughly 23 in acoustic length, depending on headjoint, bore, and embouchure.

## Public-Release Notes

- Keep all first-order geometry marked as estimated until a prototype is measured.
- Do not claim traditional equivalence from this engineering scaffold.
- Add references for any copied historic measurements before publishing them as source-backed values.
