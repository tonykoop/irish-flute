# Irish Flute D4 - Printable Shop Packet

**Readiness:** L2 scaffold. Bring `validation.csv` to the shop and fill it during the build.

## Critical Sequence

1. Scrap bore validation.
2. Tonewood bore.
3. Turn exterior from bore axis.
4. Cut embouchure and measure D4 fundamental.
5. Drill and tune one tone hole at a time.
6. Finish bore and remeasure.

## Shop Warnings

- Do not apply NAF K2 corrections.
- Do not drill all holes before measuring.
- Do not cut the body short before the embouchure speaks.
- Do not declare L3 readiness without measured validation.
