# Si Bheag Si Mhor (She Was Small, She Was Great)

**Composer:** Turlough O'Carolan (c.1720); harpist and composer (1670–1738)
**Public domain:** Yes — pre-1929 (US); O'Carolan died 1738
**Arrangement:** G major for D Irish flute. Range G4–G5.

## Range check
G4 is accessed by cross-fingering on the D flute (three fingers, left hand only).
F#5 uses the standard F# fingering (hole 5 open). All notes are achievable.

## Practice tip
O'Carolan tunes have a harp-like quality: long, singing lines. Don't break the
phrases at bar lines — let each four-bar arch build and resolve as one breath.
