# Down by the Sally Gardens

**Tradition:** Trad Irish air; W.B. Yeats poem "Down by the Sally Gardens" (1889)
**Public domain:** Yes — melody traditional pre-1900; Yeats text PD
**Arrangement:** D major for D Irish flute. Range D5–D6.

## Practice tip
A gentle song — keep the tempo unhurried (♩=72 or slower). The long notes are where
the Irish flute's woody tone shines most. Breathe fully before each phrase.
