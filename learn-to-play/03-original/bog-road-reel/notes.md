# Bog Road Reel

**Composer:** Tony Koop / Heifer Zephyr (2025)
**License:** MIT — free to perform, record, arrange, and adapt
**Form:** AABB reel, 32 bars, ♩=120
**Instrument:** D Irish flute

## Story
Somewhere between Galway and Clifden there's a road that runs along the bog — peat-black
pools, stone walls, curlews calling. This reel is meant to move at the pace of a bicycle
on that road: steady, deliberate, eyes up.

## Range check
A4–A5 (first octave of a D flute: played as A5–A6 at sounding pitch). No overblowing.

## Practice tip
Start at ♩=80. Work each part (AA, then BB) separately. Reels in sessions push toward
♩=120–140 — reach that only once the notes are effortless.
