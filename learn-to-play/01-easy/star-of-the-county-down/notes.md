# Star of the County Down

**Tradition:** Trad Irish (pre-1900); D mixolydian modal song
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional melody, pre-1929 (US)
**Arrangement:** D mixolydian for D Irish flute. Range D5–D6.

## Range check
Mostly D5–B5 with one D6. The C-natural (mixolydian b7) sits on the Irish flute's
built-in C-key — no half-holing required.

## Practice tip
Feel the mixolydian colour: the C-natural at bar 5 is the signature moment.
Play it clearly and let it ring — it's what makes this tune sound Irish.
