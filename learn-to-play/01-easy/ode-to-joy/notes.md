# Ode to Joy

**Tradition:** Beethoven, *Symphony No. 9*, Op.125 (1824)
**Public domain:** Yes — pre-1929 (US)
**Arrangement:** D major for D Irish flute. Range D5–G5 (1st octave).

## Practice tip
On the Irish flute, slower attacks give a rounder tone than a whistle.
Use this tune to practise consistent breath support through the longer note values.
