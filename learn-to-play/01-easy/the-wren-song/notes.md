# The Wren Song (An Dreoilín)

**Tradition:** Trad Irish wren-day jig (pre-1900)
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional melody
**Arrangement:** D major jig for D Irish flute. Range D5–A5 (1st octave only).

## Range check
No overblowing — stays entirely in the lower octave.

## Practice tip
A 6/8 jig: feel it as two groups of three. The wren-day tradition is a dance:
once the tune is solid, let your foot tap the dotted-crotchet pulse.
